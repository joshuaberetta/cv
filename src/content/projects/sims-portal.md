Information management support platform for IFRC surge volunteers during humanitarian emergencies.

## Overview

The SIMS Portal streamlines coordination and deployment of information management specialists during crisis response operations.

## Features

- Volunteer profile management
- Deployment tracking and coordination
- Mission resource allocation
- Real-time emergency response support

## Impact

Supports the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies (IFRC) in coordinating rapid response to disasters worldwide.
