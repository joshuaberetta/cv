Research and implementation of responsible AI solutions for humanitarian data collection in crisis contexts.

## Research Focus

Exploring how artificial intelligence and machine learning can be responsibly applied to humanitarian challenges while maintaining ethical standards and data protection.

## Key Areas

- **Responsible AI**: Ensuring AI solutions respect privacy and dignity
- **Crisis Context**: Adapting ML models for low-resource environments
- **Data Collection**: AI-assisted survey design and data quality
- **Analysis Automation**: Streamlining humanitarian needs assessments

## Principles

All work follows humanitarian principles and prioritizes the safety and privacy of affected populations.

## Collaboration

Working with international organizations to understand real-world needs and constraints in humanitarian operations.
