Leading humanitarian partnerships and technical coordination for Kobo's work with UN agencies, INGOs, and governments across crisis contexts worldwide.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Partnership Management**: Coordinate with UN agencies (UNHCR, OCHA, WFP, IOM), INGOs, and government partners
- **Technical Training**: Deliver capacity building workshops across Africa, Latin America, and Asia in multiple languages
- **Emergency Response**: Rapid deployment support for humanitarian emergencies and disasters
- **Solutions Architecture**: Design and implement data collection systems for complex humanitarian operations
- **AI Research**: Lead responsible AI integration for humanitarian data collection

## Major Projects

- Humanitarian deployment to Jamaica for Hurricane Melissa response
- Multi-agency training programs for Gates Foundation partners across Middle East and Africa
- UNHCR data collection systems for refugee and displacement contexts
- Regional capacity building for Latin American humanitarian networks

## Impact

Supporting data-driven decision making in humanitarian operations affecting millions of displaced and crisis-affected populations globally.
