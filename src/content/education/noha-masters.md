Grantee of the Erasmus Mundus scholarship for the NOHA Masters in International Humanitarian Action, a prestigious joint degree program offered by the Network on Humanitarian Action (NOHA).

## Program Overview

The NOHA Masters program is a unique international master's degree that combines academic excellence with practical field experience in humanitarian action. The program is delivered across multiple European universities, providing diverse perspectives on humanitarian challenges.

## Focus Areas

- **Humanitarian Principles & Ethics**: Core principles guiding humanitarian action
- **Emergency Response**: Crisis management and coordination in disaster contexts
- **International Humanitarian Law**: Legal frameworks governing armed conflict
- **Protection & Advocacy**: Human rights and protection in humanitarian settings
- **Program Management**: Project cycle management in humanitarian contexts
- **Research Methods**: Quantitative and qualitative research in humanitarian studies

## Academic Excellence

- Selected as Erasmus Mundus scholarship recipient (highly competitive EU-funded program)
- Multi-university program spanning Netherlands and Spain
- Level 7 qualification in the European Qualifications Framework

This advanced program bridges my technical background in humanitarian technology with strategic understanding of humanitarian action, preparing for leadership roles in international humanitarian response.
