# DIEPI Workshop - Dominican Republic

Facilitated an in-person KoboToolbox workshop for the Directorate of Epidemiology (DIEPI) in Santo Domingo.

## Workshop Details

- **Location**: Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic
- **Language**: Spanish
- **Audience**: Government epidemiology teams

## Government Collaboration

Worked directly with the Dominican Republic's national epidemiology directorate to enhance their digital data collection capabilities for disease surveillance and public health monitoring.

## Technical Focus

The workshop emphasized practical implementation of mobile data collection systems for epidemiological surveys, outbreak response, and routine health surveillance activities.
