# GPEI Workshop - Afghanistan

Conducted an in-person KoboToolbox training workshop in Kabul for the Gates Foundation and Global Polio Eradication Initiative (GPEI).

## Workshop Details

- **Venue**: IFRC/ARCS offices in Kabul
- **Duration**: Multi-day intensive workshop
- **Language**: English
- **Focus**: Mobile data collection for polio eradication programs

## Participants

Trained field teams and program managers working on polio eradication initiatives across Afghanistan, providing them with tools to improve data collection and monitoring in challenging operational environments.
