# FAO Plant Protection Workshop - Tanzania

Led a 5-day in-person training workshop in Dar es Salaam for FAO, Tanzania's National Plant Protection Organization (NPPO), and the Southern African Development Community (SADC).

## Workshop Overview

- **Duration**: 5 full days
- **Language**: English
- **Focus**: Digital data collection for plant health and agricultural monitoring

## Objectives

The workshop equipped participants with skills to digitize plant protection surveys and pest monitoring systems, improving regional capacity for agricultural surveillance and early warning systems across the SADC region.
