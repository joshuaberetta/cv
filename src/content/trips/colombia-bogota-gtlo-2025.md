# GTLo Workshop - Colombia

Delivered a comprehensive training on KoboToolbox and Introduction to AI for Humanitarians for the Grupo de Trabajo de Localización en Colombia (GTLo).

## Workshop Details

- **Location**: Bogotá, Colombia
- **Language**: Spanish
- **Topics**: Mobile data collection, AI integration, humanitarian technology

## Focus Areas

The workshop focused on empowering local humanitarian organizations with tools and knowledge to strengthen their data collection capabilities and explore responsible AI applications in their humanitarian work.
