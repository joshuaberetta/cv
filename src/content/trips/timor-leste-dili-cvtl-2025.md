# CVTL Workshop - Timor-Leste

Conducted a bilingual training workshop on KoboToolbox and Introduction to AI for Humanitarians for Cruz Vermelha de Timor-Leste (CVTL).

## Workshop Details

- **Location**: Dili, Timor-Leste
- **Languages**: English and Tetum
- **Duration**: Multi-day intensive training

## Cultural Context

Working in both English and Tetum allowed for deeper engagement with local teams, ensuring that humanitarian technology concepts were accessible to all participants regardless of their language background.

## Outcomes

Equipped Red Cross staff with practical skills in mobile data collection and introduced frameworks for responsible AI implementation in their humanitarian programs.
