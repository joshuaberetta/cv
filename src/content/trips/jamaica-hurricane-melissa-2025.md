# Hurricane Melissa Response - Jamaica

Deployed to Jamaica for 2 weeks as part of the humanitarian response and recovery efforts following Hurricane Melissa.

## Key Activities

- Collaborated with OCHA and UNDAC on emergency information management
- Supported IOM and WFP coordination efforts on the ground
- Worked with MLSS government ministry on response coordination
- Provided technical support for data collection and analysis in emergency settings

## Impact

The deployment focused on strengthening emergency IM efforts and ensuring effective coordination among humanitarian actors during the critical response phase.
