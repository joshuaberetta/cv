Facilitated a bilingual training workshop for the Timor-Leste Red Cross, combining technical skills development with responsible AI integration for humanitarian operations.

## Unique Aspects

- **Bilingual Delivery**: Conducted in both English and Tetum to ensure accessibility
- **Field Context**: Adapted content for low-connectivity environments common in Timor-Leste
- **Local Capacity Building**: Focus on sustainability and knowledge transfer

## Training Modules

1. Mobile data collection with KoboToolbox
2. Offline data collection strategies
3. Introduction to AI tools for humanitarian work
4. Data ethics and protection in sensitive contexts
5. Practical exercises with real Red Cross scenarios

## Community Impact

Strengthened the capacity of Timor-Leste Red Cross to conduct efficient needs assessments and program monitoring in remote areas.
