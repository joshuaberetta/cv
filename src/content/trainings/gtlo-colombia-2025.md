Delivered a comprehensive training combining KoboToolbox fundamentals with an introduction to responsible AI applications in humanitarian contexts for the Localization Working Group in Colombia.

## Training Components

- **KoboToolbox Fundamentals**: Survey design, mobile data collection, and analysis
- **AI for Humanitarians**: Ethical considerations and practical applications of AI in crisis response
- **Localization**: Adapting tools and approaches to local contexts
- **Data Protection**: GDPR compliance and humanitarian data principles

## Participants

Local NGOs and civil society organizations working on humanitarian response and community resilience in Colombia.

## Outcomes

Enhanced capacity of local organizations to leverage technology for more effective humanitarian programming while maintaining ethical standards.
