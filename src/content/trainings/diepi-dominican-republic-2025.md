Conducted an in-person KoboToolbox workshop for the Directorate of Epidemiology (DIEPI) in Santo Domingo, focused on building capacity for digital data collection in epidemiological surveillance.

## Workshop Focus

- Digital data collection systems for health surveillance
- Mobile data collection best practices
- Data quality and validation techniques
- Real-time reporting and analysis

## Impact

Strengthened the Dominican Republic's public health data infrastructure, enabling more efficient disease surveillance and outbreak response.
